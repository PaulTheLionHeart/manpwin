Revision History

ManpWIN has undergone continuous development over many years, with ongoing improvements to performance, precision, and fractal exploration capabilities.

Latest Release

ManpWIN 4.02d (March 2026)

  • Added Hailstone (Collatz) fractal
  • Multi-threading extended to most pixel-based fractals
  • Improved perturbation rendering and new plotting modes
  • Enhanced slope rendering performance
  • Multi-threaded formula parser
  • Numerous bug fixes and stability improvements

Previous Release

ManpWIN 4.02c

  • Initial integration of extended multi-threading framework
  • Early improvements to perturbation and slope rendering
  • General stability improvements
