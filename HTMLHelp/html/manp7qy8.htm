Revision History

ManpWIN has undergone continuous development over many years, with ongoing improvements to performance, precision, and fractal exploration capabilities.

Latest Release (April 2026)

ManpWIN 4.02f

Recent Improvements

This update improves the robustness of ManpWIN's perturbation engine and numerical stability across multiple precision modes, while introducing template-based infrastructure for future extensions.

  • Perturbation fix: corrected an issue caused by an uninitialised power value when reference generation was moved outside the CPerturbation class.
  • Rendering reliability: prevents incorrect fractal output that could appear after toggling under certain conditions.
  • Template framework: introduced template-based infrastructure, providing a foundation for extending fractal types and future perturbation support.
  • Arithmetic stability: stabilised trigonometric functions across multiple arithmetic types.
  • DD/QD improvements: corrected error handling in double-double and quad-double precision modes.
  • General stability: improved consistency for high-precision rendering, especially for cubic and higher-order fractals.
